About Us

ZMRJJ CLOTHING INFO

Welcome to ZMRJJ CLOTHING, your number one source for all things clothing. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of clothing, with an emphasis on price, quality, and convenience.

Founded in 2022 by Mark Taguba, Saladino Rasheed, Sumalo Jhonny, Vergara Joshua, Villaruel Zedrick, Loyola Adrian David, ZMRJJ CLOTHING has come a long way from its beginnings in Philippines. When the main 5 founder first started out, their passion for clothing drove them to start their own business.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
